### Hi, I'm Renato Duarte

I'm a computational neuroscientist and Principal Investigator at the [Center for Neuroscience and Cell Biology](https://www.uc.pt/cnc), University of Coimbra, where I lead the [Computational Neuroscience & Neural Computation](https://comp-neuro.org) group.

I study *how the brain computes the mind* — building mechanistic models of cortical circuits that bridge biophysics, dynamical systems theory, and cognition.

**Research interests:** cortical microcircuits, heterosynaptic plasticity, dendritic computation, sequential rule learning, dynamical systems, NeuroAI

**Technical stack:** Python, PyTorch, JAX, NEST Simulator, Arbor, HPC (SLURM/MPI)
